
From: HR Team <hr@company.com>  
To: All Staff <all-staff@company.com>  
Subject: Important Update – PTO Policy for 2026  
Date: January 10, 2026  

Dear Team,

We hope you are doing well.

We would like to inform you of an update to the Paid Time Off (PTO) policy effective immediately for 2026:

- Full-time employees are entitled to 18 PTO days per year (previously 15 days)
- PTO accrues monthly at a rate of 1.5 days per month
- Employees may carry over up to 5 unused PTO days into the next calendar year
- Any unused PTO beyond this limit will be forfeited at year end

Please note that contractors are not eligible for PTO benefits.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact HR.

Kind regards,  
HR Team

As part of our ongoing commitment to security, we are updating our mandatory requirements:
