# Company policies (2026)

This page summarizes key internal policy communications found in `docs/company-knowledge/company-policies.md`.

## Paid time off (PTO)
- **Eligibility**: Full-time employees only (contractors are not eligible).
- **Allowance**: **18 PTO days/year**.
- **Accrual**: **1.5 days/month**.
- **Carryover**: Up to **5 unused days** into the next calendar year.
- **Forfeiture**: Unused PTO beyond carryover limit is **forfeited at year end**.

## Remote work
- **Default allowance**: Up to **3 days/week** remote with manager approval.
- **Location restriction**: Remote work must be performed **within your country of employment**.
- **Working from another country**: Requires approval from **Manager, HR, and Legal**.

## Security requirements
- **MFA**: **Required for all systems and accounts**.
- **Password length**: Minimum **12 characters**.
- **Password sharing**: **Prohibited**.
- **Incident reporting**: Report suspected incidents to `security@company.com` immediately.

## Payroll
- **Pay schedule**: Monthly on the **last business day**.
- **Payment method**: **Bank transfer only**.
- **Contractors**: Paid through the **invoice system**.
- **Issue reporting window**: Report salary issues within **30 days** of payment date.

## Intern equipment
- **Provided**: Company laptop for the internship period.
- **Not automatically provided**: Monitors and other accessories.
- **Return**: Equipment must be returned at program completion.
